Welcome to the EveryDay G.I.T.A Challenge!

History and Context

ॐ श्री गुरुभ्यो नमः

With the blessings of Sri Krishna, Gita Maata, and our revered Gurus, Sri K.V. Shivaswamy and Dr. Venkata Raju, we are delighted to begin the second cycle of the EveryDay G.I.T.A. (Everyday Gita In Text and Audio) Yagna. This initiative began on May 17th, 2022, after nearly five months of daily sessions with Dr. Raju. We successfully completed the first cycle on April 26th, 2024.

Each day, participants receive a verse from the Bhagavad Gita along with an audio recitation by one of our dedicated volunteers.

At the conclusion of every chapter, we invite youth and others to participate in the EveryDay G.I.T.A Challenge, offering an opportunity to share their own audio or video recitations and connect with the teachings of the Gita.

How to Submit Your Entry

To participate in the EveryDay G.I.T.A Challenge, please follow these steps:

  1. Record and share the whole chapter or a verse or two that resonates with you.
  2. Include your picture.
  3. Provide your name.
  4. If you are a student, include your class/age; if you are an adult, include your profession.
  5. Submit your audio recording in mp3 or m4a format, or a video in mp4 format if preferred.

We deeply appreciate all participants, as well as the teachers, parents, and supporters who encourage their students and children to engage with this challenge. Special thanks to Anika , a college student, for her valuable contribution in setting up the website.
